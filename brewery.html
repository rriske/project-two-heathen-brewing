This is where the magic happens. Our production brewery is where you can come for tastings, pick-up a keg, or even bottles. We offer online purchase options and when your beer is ready, you can come on down and pick it up. If that’s not for you, you can always come on down and check us out in person!
We also rent out keg supplies from our production brewery. A small security deposit, plus a $10 rental fee will get you the setup you need.
Kids are welcome and we try to make it a fun visit for them. We’ll also hook them up with a free Heathen Root Beer.
